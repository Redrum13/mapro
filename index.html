About the Map

The Walk map for the English Garden was made by the creators with one goal in mind: To reduce the decision-making time of finding which route to take for a walk.
The estimated time and distance can be seen for all predefined routes along with the nearest transport stations. There are also points of monuments, click on them to know more to.
This simplified map is for all those who are sick of drowning in information and want straight-forward no brainer solutions. Zoom in to see all the paths and interesting points within the park, browse multiple routes at a glance and find your best fit.

Click on the Tracks button and check the route name. Then Select a track to zoom in. You can always uncheck the tracks that do not interest you.
