EXPLORE

The English Garden in Munich is the largest inner-city park in the world.
Explore the Chinese Tower, stroll along the Isar, or take in the breathtaking panoramic views from the Monopteros. With every step, you'll uncover a new story, a quiet moment, or a vibrant gathering spot. Whether you're meandering through the woods in the quieter northern section or marveling at the playful surfers on the Eisbach wave, the park offers something for everyone.

So, lace up your walking shoes and let the journey begin.

About
the Map

The Walk map for the English Garden was made by the creators with one goal in mind: To reduce the decision-making time of finding which route to take for a walk.
The time, distance and predefined routes along with the nearest transport stations are given. There are points of interest, click on them to know more. The GPS function allows you to navigate along the path and there is a measurement tool to calculate distance from a transport station where the route is not defined.
Zoom in to see all the paths and interesting points within the park, browse multiple routes at a glance and find your best fit.

Click on the Tracks button and check the route name. Then select a track to zoom in. You can always uncheck the tracks that do not interest you.
